Hello everyone,
 
I hope you all had a good start to the winter semester!
To make sure you can secure a spot in our popular courses early, here’s an overview of the upcoming events and workshops organized by Kulturwerk Lüneburg:
 
October 30 – Free Painting in Mixed Media (Building 16, 3:00–6:00 p.m.)
In the mood for color? Combine watercolors, pastels, pencils, and tempera – all materials provided. Perfect for spontaneous creatives.
 
November 4 – Wood Workshop (WerkStadt Lüneburg, 4:00–8:00 p.m.)
Introduction to the circular saw & build your own box – limited spots available.
 
November 13 & 27 – Experimental Print Workshop (Building 16, 3:00–6:00 p.m.)
Experiment with linoleum printing inks, small rollers, and styrene sheets. You can book one or both sessions.
 
November 13, 20 & 27 – Dance in Connection (Room of Silence, 6:00–8:30 p.m.)
Nonviolent communication meets movement – explore mindfulness, body awareness, and empathy through guided exercises and exchange.
 
November 18 – Wood Workshop: Carving Basics (WerkStadt Lüneburg, 4:00–8:00 p.m.)
Spoon carving for beginners – only a few spots left.
 
December 2 – Oil Painting: Still Life (Building 16, 3:00–6:00 p.m.)
Working with water-soluble oil paints.
 
December 4 – Mold Casting (Transformationsräume, 4:00–6:00 p.m.)
Create your own pieces with Raysin – limited spots available.
 
December 9 – Winter Creative Workshop (Transformationsräume, 4:00–6:00 p.m.)
Design sparkling sun catchers, lovely macramé art, and handmade greeting cards.
 
In January, you can also look forward to a portrait painting course, an upcycling workshop (“Sculpting with Paper”), and a pottery workshop.
The pottery workshop is already fully booked – you can join the waiting list, and we may add a second date.
 
Participation in all workshops is free of charge, and all materials are provided.
For more information, visit: https://stw-on.de/lüneburg/kultur.
 
If you’d like to take part, just reply to this email and mention the workshop of your choice.
 
If you no longer wish to receive updates from Kulturwerk (the next email will not come before the summer semester), please reply with “No, thank you,” and you’ll be removed from the mailing list right away.
